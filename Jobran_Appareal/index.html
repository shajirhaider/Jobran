About Us

Jobran Apparel was established to provide high-quality Garment products for major brands and retailers worldwide. Our focus is to satisfy our valuable customers by providing high-quality products while maintaining a competitive price. We source our products from a wide range of supply chains including our own production house. As a result, we can meet the demand of the diverse markets including customised products, which brought us the trust of various international brands. We also pay huge attention to our sources regarding CSR (Corporate Social Responsibilities) and our company ethics that’s why zero-tolerance policy and UK anti-bribery policy is applied. Customers satisfaction is essential at Jobran Apparel that’s why we treat each Garment as a piece of art that represents the company reputation.

Our Policy

1

Child labour

Child labour is not tolerated. We reject working with sources and partners who employ children. All workers must be over the mandatory school-going age in the respective country’s operation. Furthermore, it is our obligation to support workplace apprenticeship programs as an educational program.

2

Disciplinary practises

All our business partners shall obey instigate their disciplinary action with respect to the local law. We reject All sort of abusive action and the practice of physical or mental abuse or any form of coercive practice against our source’s workers.

3

Environment

sustainability and stewardship is in jobran’s DNA , we ensure that none of our business partners production process harms or affects the environment negatively at any cost any case . hat’s why all our business partners are obliged to meet all the legal criteria of operating production activities according to law .

4

Unauthorized Subcontract

Unauthorised subcontracting is considered a Zero Tolerance Violation according to Jobran’s policy. No vendors can subcontract any aspect of production without prior information to and approval from the group companies. Any violation will result in a delisting of such factories

5

Ethical standards

we try to distinguish and work with business partners who have a similar ethical standard with us.

6

Health and safety

all factories that work with us have to offer a safe environment for their workers and the highest safety procedures shall be implemented.

7

Dormitories

it's necessary for any of our business partners who provide a residential facility to their workers to ensure its hygienic and safe.

8

Legal requirement

our business partner must implement and comply with the local laws in relation to the conduct of their business.

9

Discrimination

Fairness and equality are a part of Jobran’ s believe, for that reason, we ensure that workers are given the opportunity to work based on their skills only. No discrimination due to cultural, religious, gender or any other differences is accepted at any circumstances.

10

Freedom of association

we ensure our sources give their workers the right to join an association of their choice and their right to collective barging. Moreover, those workers shall not be discriminated or enforced by any sort of disciplinary action if they do not violate the local law.

11

Wages and benefits

Jobran apparel strictly monitors the worker's welfare and for that reason, we only work with such business partners who provide their workers legally fixed wages, compensation for overtime and all the benefits set by the local law

12

Working Time

Our business partners always try and meet the 60 hours (per week) limit and whenever that crosses they compensate their workers for additional hours according to local law. We don’t work with business partners who make their workers work more than 60 hours (weekly ) on a regular basis.
